Turn-Based Strategy Game Prototype

About the Project

This prototype is a turn-based strategy game featuring a variety of gameplay mechanics designed to provide an engaging and tactical experience. Although the project is not yet complete, it showcases several core functionalities that form the foundation of a complex strategy game.

Key Features:

  • Grid-Based Movement: Allows characters to move across a grid, providing a strategic element to positioning.
  • Shooting Mechanics: Implement ranged attacks with range weapons.
  • Melee Attacks: Engage in close combat with melee weapons.
  • Grenade Throwing: Use grenades to attack multiple enemies within a specific area.
  • Destroyable Objects: Interact with the environment by destroying objects that can alter the battlefield.
  • Interactable Objects: Includes objects like doors that players can open and close, adding another layer of strategy.

The prototype uses a grid system for movement and positioning, where players can move their units to different grid squares. Combat includes both ranged and melee attacks, with animations and effects. The game also supports destructible environments and throwable objects, adding layers of strategy and interactivity. Interactable objects such as doors can be opened or closed, impacting movement and line of sight.
