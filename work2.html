Kubota Farm (Oculus Quest)

About the Project

"Kubota Farm" for Oculus Quest offers an immersive 360-degree video experience, allowing users to explore and learn about Kubota farms. With a friendly Kubota mascot as a guide, users can delve into the details of farm operations and equipment, all while enjoying a personalized journey.

Key Features:

  • 360-Degree Video: Dive into the world of Kubota farms with immersive 360-degree videos, offering a lifelike and interactive experience.
  • Kubota Mascot Guide: Interact with a charming Kubota mascot who provides information and insights about the farm, equipment, and operations.
  • Personalized Content: The experience keeps track of user preferences and interests, tailoring the content to each user's preferences and displaying the most-watched videos.
  • Rating System: Users can rate videos, helping to curate content and provide feedback for an improved viewing experience.
  • Data Analytics: The system collects data on user preferences and viewing habits, allowing for continuous improvement and content optimization.

"Kubota Farm" on Oculus Quest combines education and entertainment, offering a unique and engaging way to learn about Kubota farms and equipment.


Interactive House (WebGL)

About the Project

The WebGL project "Interactive House" offers a dynamic experience where users can customize and visualize different materials within a virtual house model. Through a user-friendly interface, users can select and apply various materials to different parts of the house, triggering animations and visual changes corresponding to their selections.

Key Features:

  • Interactive Material Selection: Choose from a range of materials such as wood, brick, marble, and more using an intuitive UI.
  • Real-time Visualization: Witness instant visual changes as selected materials are applied to different components of the house model.
  • Dynamic Animations: Experience dynamic animations that showcase how materials interact with light and environment, enhancing realism.
  • Customization Options: Customize the appearance of walls, floors, roofs, and other elements within the house, allowing for creative exploration.
  • User-Friendly Interface: The UI provides a seamless and easy-to-use experience, making material selection and customization accessible to all users.

"Interactive House" leverages WebGL technology to deliver an engaging and interactive platform for users to explore material options and visualize their design preferences within a virtual environment.
