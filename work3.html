About the Project

"The tower defense game project offers an immersive gaming experience where players defend against waves of enemies using strategic placement of towers. The game includes a watch ad system for rewards, in-app purchases, and receipt validation through a Node.js backend.

Responsibilities:

  • Implement Watch Ad System:
    • Develop a system allowing players to watch ads in exchange for rewards such as in-game currency, power-ups, or cosmetic items.
    • Ensure a cooldown timer is managed by the backend to regulate the frequency of ad watches for each player.
  • Integrate In-App Purchases:
    • Set up secure payment gateways for players to purchase in-game items, upgrades, or currency.
    • Design and implement a user-friendly interface for browsing and purchasing items.
  • Develop Receipt Validation:
    • Create a backend service to validate purchase receipts sent by the game client.
    • Ensure the validation process confirms the genuineness and integrity of each purchase.
  • Handle Item Distribution:
    • Upon receipt validation, develop functionality to distribute purchased items or currency to the playerâ€™s account in real-time.
    • Ensure players receive their items instantly to enhance their gaming experience.
  • Create Sound System:
    • Develop a dynamic sound system that enhances the gaming experience with background music, sound effects, and voice-overs.
    • Retrieve sound data from the backend, allowing for updates and customization based on game events or player actions.
    • Ensure the sound system integrates seamlessly with other game elements and adjusts dynamically to in-game events.
  • Fix Bugs:
    • Identify and resolve bugs reported by users or found during testing.
    • Ensure all bug fixes maintain game stability and performance.
    • Conduct thorough testing after fixes to prevent regressions.

Security and Reliability:

  • Encrypt sensitive data transmissions, including receipts and sound data, to ensure security.
  • Implement anti-fraud measures and rate limiting to prevent abuse and maintain fairness.
  • Log all transactions, errors, and sound system events comprehensively for effective monitoring and debugging.

The tower defense game leverages modern technologies and backend infrastructure to deliver a seamless and enjoyable gaming experience with rewarding progression options for players.
